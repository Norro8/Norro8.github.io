May 14th

Today was the day we traveled to Kyoto.

First thing in the morning we took a train down to Tokyo Station to catch a bullet train.

Tokyo Station was huge and it was very confusing on where to go / what to do to catch the train. Eventually we made it to the right track and were on our way to Kyoto.

Looking out the window was interesting since there were many small towns and mountains. (add picture)

Once we arrived we took 2 local trains to get to our next hotel.

We dropped our heavy bags off at the hotel and wondered down to a cafe we has heard about.

The area was very nice and had many old buildings.

After the cafe, we walked down to a main road to look for shopping stores.

Kyoto was quite different here than Tokyo. It had so many tour groups and the stores looked more like home.

After shopping around for a bit we headed back to our hotel for the night.
