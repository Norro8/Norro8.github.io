May 10th

Big day today we started by walking to Masashino Mori Diner for breakfast.

It was lightly raining most of the morning but stopped after we were done breakfast.

We then just wandered around the area near Shinjuku Station until we found the 3D cat billboard. Unfortunatly cameras can't capture it very well.

Next we headed over to a small coffee place. I got a cappuccino there. It was very small inside.

After that we wondered around a bit more before going to a shrine.

It was around 4:00pm and we had been walking pretty much the entire time. We noticed we were close to Shibuya crossing (about 30mins away walking) so instead of heading back to rest a bit we decided to keep going as we probably wouldn't have another chance to see it.

Shibuya area was quite a bit busier so getting there was a bit difficult.

At this point we were starting to get hungery so we started trying to find a place to eat. It took a bit but we ended up finding a burger place.

It was starting to get dark but we made it to Shibuya crossing. My legs and feet were hurting quite a bit.

From here it was about 1 hour 20 mins walk back.

Once we got back I just went to bed. I was too tired to do anythong else.

End of May 10th
