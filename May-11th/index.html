May 11th

Another big day today. We started by going back to Masashino Mori Diner for breakfast again.

We then worked on figuring out how to use the train. Once we found the right line we took the train to Akihabara.

When we got there we wondered around for a bit before getting to the main street where there was some sort of festival going on.

It was insanely busy and you could barely walk. We decided to find a new coffee place and ended up at Vault coffee and toast. It was just down a small ally close to the main street.

Just like the other coffee place, it was quite small. We got a window seat so we could still see the festival. We ordered a coffee and cinnemon toast. The coffee was ok but the cinnemon toast was really good.

At this point the festival had moved down the road to a different area so it was much less busy. We walked around a couple of stores for a while. There were entire floors based an anime, games, and figures!

We wanted to come back when it got dark and a friend told us about a massive shopping center just a couple blocks away. Since we sill had a couple hours before dark, thats where we went next.

The building was gigantic! It had 8 floors and each had so many different things!

My leg had started hurting from all of the walking and once we got back outside we decided just to go find a place to eat supper.

We were looking for a restaurant that had a lighter menu (we were not so hungery from the toast) but couldn't find anything around our current area. We ended up going to 7-11 since we heard it was different in japan.

We found that 7-11 WAS very different and they had salads, rice balls, and sandwiches! Our entire meal only cost 10$!

It was finally getting dark so we went to look at the main street once more. It was definitely worth waiting.

After that we took a train back to our place and went to bed. Between today and yesterday we had walked more than 35km! No wonder my legs and feet were hurting!
